A True Muslim

The Holy Prophet (PBUH) describes a true Muslim in these words:

”A true Muslim is one who does not defame or abuse others, but he becomes a refuge for mankind, their lives and their properties”

The Holy Quran says:

“Verily the true religion with Allah is Islam.”

Allah has created the universe including man, then who may know about the needs of man better than the creator Himself? As guidance is the spiritual need of man Allah guided the man for this purpose. He sent many prophets. They did the work to guide the man. The last and the greatest among them is our Holy prophet Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH). He taught the favorite message of Allah Islam. The Holy Prophet (PBUH) said:

“Allah creates every person on the nature of Islam. These are the parents who turn into a Christian or a Jew”

Islam is a true religion. It is a complete code of life. The followers of Islam called “Muslims”. As it is said in poem,

“We aim for highest,

Praying for longest.

Asking for forgiveness,

To the fullest.

Thinking about the heaven,

Is the sweetest.

Taking the path of rightest.

Living life is toughest.

Fighting the game like bravest,

I am “Muslim.”

Never can you fade us,

Come along and join us.”

A true Muslim is the one who follows the principles of Islam fully. He possesses all the constructive traits. It is said,

“He prays best who loves all”

He has firm faith in Allah, His Prophets, angels, books and the day of resurrection. He also believes in good or bad fate. After having this firm faith, he says prayers five times a day. He pays “zakat and performs “Hajj”. He fasts during the whole month of ‘Ramazan’. The life of a true Muslim reflects true spirit of Islam. He never violates the golden principles of Islam. As it is said,

“When wealth is lost, nothing is lost.

When health is lost, something is lost.

When character is lost, everything is lost.”

His dealings and conduct with people are always exemplary. He holds a high standard of morality A true Muslim is not a wealth seeker. He is contented with what he earns from his noble labour. The wealth of the whole world cannot give him peace and contentment. His peace and contentment lie in bowing before Allah. As Holy Quran says:

“Verily the remembrance’ of Allah is the only source of peace for hearts.”

A true Muslim never ignores the directions showed by our beloved Holy prophet (PBUH). He respects others and is respected by others. He remains polite and never harms others. The Holy Quran says:

“A Muslim is the one from whose hands and tongue others Muslims are safe.”

He performs his duties magnificently. He meets all his obligations in respect of his parents, family, relatives and country. It is said:

“Prayer is the voice of faith.”

A true Muslim is like a flower that gives its fragrance even to the hands that crushes it. He does not try to run after him who tries to avoid him. A true Muslim believes that

“A wise men first thinks and then speaks but a fool speaks first and then thinks.”

A true Muslim is neither a miser nor a spendthrift. He maintains balance between these two extremes. He is thankful to Allah in prosperity. He shows patience in adversity. The Holy Quran says:

“Verily, Allah is with patients.”

He knows that the joys and sorrows of this world are temporary. He knows that the purpose of his life is to please Allah. The Holy Quran highlights it as:

” Do good to others, definitely, Allah loves those who do good to others.”

Knowledge is obligatory in Islam. A true Muslim learns from lap of his mother to his grave. Our Holy Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) advised to gain knowledge in the following words.

“It is the duty of every Muslim to gain knowledge”

Moreover,

“A man of wealth has many enemies while a man of knowledge has many friends “

(Imam Ali (AS) Ibn-e-Abu Talib)

He is always broadminded. He ponders over the secrets of universe. He ever struggles to discover the hidden treasures of universe. As Holy Quran says:

“Visit on the earth and see.”

He never feels hesitant in adopting and using modern technologies. In short, to be a good Muslim is true, a great blessing of Allah. A true Muslim possesses all positive and constructive qualities. He is beneficial and useful to his family, society and country. But in doing all this he never ignores the spirit of Islam.

“MAY ALLAH BLESS US TO BE A TRUE MUSLIM!”
