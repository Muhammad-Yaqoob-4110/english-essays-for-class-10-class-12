My Aim in Life

"The ambition if reached or not makes great the life"

(Robert Browning)

Aristotle maintains that man is a goal seeking animal. His life becomes meaningful only when he struggles for his goals. Many people fail in life because they fail to organize their energies and focus on something. People, like nails, lose their effectiveness when they lose direction and begin to bend.

"Not failure but low aim is a crime"

In this universe Allah has created nothing without purpose as the Holy Quran says:

“And we have created not the heaven and the earth and that is between them in vain.”

Likewise, every human bas been sent on this universe for a particular purpose Allah granted the man with the qualities of brain and heart. So, it is the responsibility of every to understand himself and person determine a particular aim for him. As it is said:

“The aim of the wise is not to secure pleasure but to avoid pain.”

Because, aim is as important life as destination is important for ship. It is said:

“Shoot for the moon, even if you miss, you will land among the stars.”

Ambition works as a guiding star, sharpens the abilities and faculties of a person. Ambition simply means aim or desire for one’s future. It helps as in keeping our attention focused on a particular point. As it is said, without ambition life would be as.

“A Life without soul,

A head without brain,

A flower without smell,

A lamp without light.”

It motivates us. It forces us to be honest, hardworking and steadfast in our efforts. There are different aims set by the people.

“If you want to reach the highest, begin at the lowest.”

(hakespeare)

“All the progress and prosperity achieved in the world is the result of human ambitions.”

It has converted the world into a global village. Instant and the swiftest of communication have changed our lifestyle altogether. The reason why I want to be an expert in Information Technology is quite simple.

“There are two things to aim at in life. First, to get what You want and after that to enjoy it. Only the wisest of mankind achieve the second.”

By adopting this profession, I would be able to refute all charges falsely levelled against “Islam” Beside this I would be able to attract the non- Muslims towards Islam through internet and E-mails. As it is said:

“If You can imagine it, you can achieve it. If you can dream It, you can become it.”

I work day and night to achieve my goal and to enjoy it. I am Sure that I will become an expert in Information Technology after four years.

“If you can’t fly then run.

If you can’t run then walk.

If you can’t walk then crawl.

But whatever you do you have to keep moving forward.”

I have special aptitude and talent for this field. I know it is difficult to achieve my goal but I still believe that.

“Nothing is in is impossible in this world, the word itself says I’m possible!”

“The aim reached or not, make great the life.”

“And a man earns whatever he makes effort for.”

In the final analysis, I must say that aims and ambitions are very important to determine a career or profession for us. I am ready to perform any sacrifice for it. I am determined to leave no stone unturned to achieve my aim in life.
