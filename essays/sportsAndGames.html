Sports and Games

The Greeks were the first nation who introduced the marathon race and told the ways how to shape the body through exercise and sports. They believed that:

“One man practicing sportsmanship is far better than fifty preaching it.”

So,

“Sports and games are essential for Life and also a part of education.”

In past, sports were regarded as outdoor recreational pastimes. Such as fishing, hunting and shooting. On the other hand, games were the organized contests played by individuals or the teams. But distinction between these two terms disappeared with the passage often of time. Now both terms are often used interchangeably. Games are common to all countries and cultures. As it is said,

“Cultures of different countries are differed from one another but games are same to be all of them.“

Sports and games have gained cardinal importance in physical and recreational activities of life. People, play games either, to have fun or to develop their mental or physical skills. As it is said,

“Sports increase the moral and social values of life”

Their worth has been recognized at international level. In educational institutions they have been declared a part of curricula. As it is said,

“Mere Literary and academic life is not necessary.”

Sports and games are very important for students. Because only a healthy student can take an active part in activities of life.

“Game are as important in schools as the books are.“

(Imam Ghazali)

Some bookworms give no importance to sports. They are always absorbed in their studies. Then ultimately, they lose their mental and physical health. They should know that there is no substitution for health.

“If there is no adversity, health is great wealth.”

(Ghalib)

Sports are very necessary for their studies. As Holy Quran says:

“A sound body has a sound mind”

Sports and games have many advantages. They are essential to be successful in Life. If a man works all the time and gives no importance to sports and games, he cannot be successful in life. As it is said,

“Sports and games are persevered of Success”

Moreover,

“All work and no play, makes Jack a dull boy.”

Sports and games are very important for our health. Games like football, hockey and cricket keep our joints maintained. As it is said,

“Cricket by practice

Hockey by power

Football by chance

Tennis by dance”

Our blood pressure remains at constant level, and we become healthy So, we can “live” not just “exist.”

“The first wealth is health.”
(Emerson)

Games make us hardworking, practical and disciplined. They teach us that if we want victory, we have to work, work and work. Because,

“The more you swept in practice the less you bleed in battle”

The youth of today want to assert themselves. They want some outlet to present their unrivalled faculties. Sports and games provide them with plenty of such opportunities. If these chances are not given to them, they may fall a prey to criminal activities. So, indirectly sports contribute a lot towards making our youth decent and upright. As a result, undertake beneficial pursuits. As it is said,

“The ideal of a good and perfect Life is to have a sound mind in a sound body.”

Players obey the order of their captain and the umpire without any question. They have training in getting the defeat with a good heart. They have given the lesson,

“You can learn little lesson from victory but you can learn everything from defeat.”

Games also teach us discipline. People learn to obey and uphold laws. Games promote team spirit in players. They keep them united. They forget their tiny differences and fight for a common cause. They learn to co- operate. They show an infinite patience in their doings. But!

“Excess of everything is harmful.”

There is a dark side of this picture also. Games may cause certain harms sometimes the players do ignore the rules of decency for their own victory. They aim to win by hook or crook. They practice illegal and immoral tricks to this end. Second, too much interest may spoil some intelligent students. They may ignore their studies to become good athletes. In this way, they become neither good students nor good athletes. But,

“Advantages are more than dis-advantages”

One thing must be kept in mind that the spirit of games is not to win only. Games also teach us to practice, patience, tolerance, honesty, dignity and other moral values.

“If You are going to win, win gracefully.

If you are going to love, love faithfully.

If you are going to learn, learn by heart.

If you are going to fall, fall by reaching.”

Sports are very interesting pastime. They should be organized that they will not interfere with studies of our youth. Life is not all sports; sports should be an only a part of it.

To conclude, sports are healthy activity to create a healthy society. They are an expression of players' stamina, athleticism, sportsman spirit and self-control. Besides, sports are great fun for players as well as spectators.

"What I know most surely about morality and the duty of man, I owe it to sport."

(Albert Camus)
