My Favourite Book

Charles William Eliot an American academic, said:

"Books are the quietest and most constant of friends; they are the most accessible and wisest of counselors, and the most patient of teachers."

Acquiring knowledge has always been man's top most priority. Books are really a superb source of knowledge. They soothe us in sorrow and solace us in sadness. They are an invaluable asset for humans. As it is said:

“Laws dies books never.”

They prove to be sincere companions in solitude and seclusion. Our character building depends mainly upon the right selection of books. As it is said:

“A great book is the best of friends, the same today and forever. “

Ever since my childhood, I have read a lot of books, short stories, novels, works of poets and biographies have been of great interest to me. But one book has interested me the most and that is “Holy Quran”.

“No doubt. It is a steering book for piety people.”

Holy Quran is the Holy book of Allah. It is the guiding star not only for Muslims but also for disbelievers. It consists of 30 chapters and 114 Surah and 6666 verses. It is Said:

“A good book is a precious life blood of a master’s spirit.”

The Holy Quran is the book of wisdom. It is the source of guidance for all. It is not an ordinary book. It is said:

“Some books are to be tasted, others to be Swallowed and a few to be chewed digested.”

The Holy Quran was revealed our Holy Prophet (PBUH) about fourteen hundred years ago. Since then, it has been intact and unharmed. None can change even a verse from it. Allah has Himself undertaken the work of its safety. Allah says in the Holy Quran:

“Verily, we it is we, who have sent down this “Zikr” (Quran) and surely, we will guard it. “

It is the miracle of the Holy Quran that a Muslim can learn and remember it easily. Every Muslim in the world read it daily. Millions of people learn it correctly and distinctively. Some people learn it by heart. They are called “Hafiz-e-Quran”. Holy Quran was not sent as a whole as Allah says in Holy Quran:

“Verily we have sent the Holy Quran to you in segments.”

The companions of the Holy Prophet (PBUH) collected the parts on which verses were written and gave them the shape of a book. The Holy Quran contains truth and realities only. It is a complete code of life. Its teachings are evergreen and eternal. It is Said:

“There is no friend as loyal as book.”

It is an ultimate source of guidance and instructions. It gives an exhaustive commentary on religion, ethics, politics, science, logic, philosophy, psychology, economics and nature. It has rules and regulations to lead a successful life. Its teachings give birth to strong character and good habbits. It urges man to ponder over universe and discover new and novel Laws of nature. As Holy Quran says:

“Visit on the earth and see.”

Each and every aspect of life has been discussed in it. It highlights all the branches of knowledge. It is said:

“The entire known world excepting only Savage nations are governed by books.”

The Holy Quran is the message of peace and amity. It teaches parity, justice, honesty, dignity, truthfulness and piety. It lays concrete foundations for a civilized and cultured society. It ends discrimination on the basis of cast colour or race. It says that all men are equal. They are offspring of Hazrat Adam (AS). The honoured and successful are those who are virtuous and honest. An exhaustive discussion on belief, worship, business and morality is the subject of Holy Quran. The stories of various prophets have also been discussed in it. Nothing can replace this book by its words and reality. So,

“To imagine a world without books is to imagine a world without thoughts, feelings, compassion, history and voice”

To conclude, "The Holy Quran" not only enriched my heart and soul, but refined my language skills also.
