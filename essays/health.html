Health

“Health is wealth”

It is a good and famous proverb we heard from our elders. Health is a great blessing of Allah upon us. We can adopt many devices to maintain our health. It is said:

“Half the spiritual difficulties that men and women suffer arise from the morbid state of health.”

Health is a mental and physical well-being. It is a gift of Allah. A healthy man enjoys the beauties of nature. He leads a happy and delightful life. Nothing can rejoice or relax an ailing man. Such a man loses all the charms of life. Life becomes dull, drab and dreary for him. He himself becomes an unbearable burden on others. Sometimes, he desires to die to avoid the pangs of such a painful life. Therefore, nothing is more precious than health. It is said:

“The feeling of health can only be gained by sickness.”

The importance of good health can be known by illness. Health is a divine blessing. Sick people cannot do anything of their own taste. They have to depend on others. Rest is also very important for health. It is said:

“Sleep is the golden chain that ties health and our bodies together.”

But a healthy person can do anything of his own wish and desire. Therefore, man should pay an utmost heed to health. It is said:

“Prevention is better than cure.”

For this purpose, man should live in the environment conductive to health. He should take nutritious food. Because,

“The ideal of a good health is to have a sound mind in a Sound body.”

Good food, calm atmosphere, fresh air, pure water, regular physical exercise and better sanitary conditions are bare essential health.

First balanced diet is absolutely necessary for health. Human beings require food to grow, reproduce and maintain health. Without food, our bodies cannot stay warm. They cannot build or repair tissues. Good food helps us to prevent certain diseases or recover fast from illness. Our food contains certain chemicals substances called “nutrients.”

These nutrients help our body to perform its important functions. They are classified as carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals and water. Carbohydrates, proteins and fats provide the body with the energy it needs to be perform its functions. We should get up early in the morning. It is said:

“Early to bed and early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy and wise.”

Second, regular physical exercise makes a man healthy, fit, active, agile and smart. It is said,

“Walking is the best exercise for longer life.”

It is also said:

“To enjoy the glow of good health you must exercise.”

Being a Muslim if we act upon the golden principles and teachings of our Holy Prophet (PBUH) , We will be healthy. Our Holy Prophet (PBUH) said:

“Your body has rights on you.”

Therefore, it is our duty to keep us healthy. Drinking and smoking are the bad habits that are even fatal. Young people have plenty of energy. They should take part in physical activities. Moreover, another big source of health is “cleanliness”. Because, it saves us from many diseases. Our Holy Prophet (PBUH) said:

“Cleanliness is the half part of faith.”

For this purpose, we should take a bath daily. We should keep our body as well as surrounding clean. We should clean our teeth daily.

Health is very important for students because only a heathy student can take an active part in activities of life. Some bookworms pay no heed to their mental and physical fitness. They are always absorbed in their studies ultimately; they lose their mental and physical fitness. They should know that there is no substitution to health.

“If there is no adversity, health is great wealth.”

(Ghalib)

Moreover, Holy Quran says:

“A sound body has a sound mind.”

The improvement of health of entire population is first and foremost duty of government. Government should provide the people with better sanitary conditions and protect them from health hazards. It is said:

“The fate of a nation has often depended on good or bad digestion of a prime minister.”

Government should provide the people with better health facilities, good food and calm atmosphere should be ensured. Government should educate the people about health. It should provide the people with high quality health Services. One thing must be kept in mind that nothing is more precious than health. So, everyone should lead a happy and healthy life.

"Nor love, nor honour, wealth nor power,

Can give the heart a cheerful hour,

When time is lost. Be timely wise;

With health all taste of pleasure files."
