My Best Friend

In the Words of Nicole Richie:

“Ture friends are like diamonds bright, beautiful, valuable, and always in style.”

In our lives, we come across many people. We see new faces every day. Friends come and go. throughout our life. People today have become so selfish that it becomes difficult to regard anybody as your best friend. People whom you regard as your best friends often prove otherwise.

"Friends are like melons

Shall I tell you why?

To Find a good one, You must a hundred try."
(Claude Mermet)

A friend is an invaluable asset. They enhance our happiness and soothe our sorrows. They stand by us through thick and thin. As it is said:

“Chances make parents. But choices make Friends so, A friend is a sheltering tree”

They are a source joy and pleasure for us. We can share our joy and sorrow with them. Bacon says:

“No one can live without friend, except beast or a God.”

Moreover, he says:

“A person who has no friend eats up his own heart.”

A person who has no friend feels alone. He always remains sad. A friend is a precious diamond. A person who has no friend is poor. As the Holy Quran says:

“He who has no friend is poor.”

A person who has no friend cannot share his joys and sorrows with others. Therefore, to have a false friend is better than to have no friend.

“A friend is the one before whom I can think aloud.”

I have many friends but Ali Ahmed is my best friend. He is my neighbour and class fellow. We usually play and study together. He is a sharp and energetic boy. I like his for his jolly nature and witty remarks. As it is said:

“Friendship is a nice relationship more precious than gold.”

In evening we go for a walk together. He can understand my feelings. He is very co-operative. He helps me in study. In short, He possesses all the traits of a good friend. As it is said:

“A true friend is one soul in two bodies.”

He possesses all the qualities of a balanced personality. He has a muscular physique and enjoys good health.He takes exercise regularly. He plays football in the evening.He is the best athlete of our school. He believes that

“Health is Wealth.”

He is very faithful and sincere friend. As it is said:

“There are many fair-weather friends but it is difficult to find a faithful friend.”

He is active, agile, sharp, energetic and strong. His power and vigour are exemplary. He takes active part in co-curricular activities of school. He is a good debater also. He has won many competitions of quiz and debates. In short, He is a “Live wire”. He is religious minded girl/boy. He loves his religion more than anything else. He observes all the Islamic Principles. He says prayer regularly. He reads the Holy Quran and acts upon its teachings. He takes an active part in religious activities. He is polite and well mannered. As it is said:

“Reprove your friends in secret and praise them openly.”

He also motivates me towards good habits. As it is said:

“Reprove your friends in secret and praise them openly.”

His behaviour with others is matchless. All people love him for his loving nature and warm feelings. He is also a good social worker. He is a successful and good coordinator. He helps the school staff in arranging functions.

“Friends are like melons Shall I tell you why? To find one good you must try a hundred.”

He proves the proverb:

“The friend in need is a friend indeed.”

Helen Keller a great writer highlights the importance of a friend in these words,

“I would rather walk with a friend in the dark than alone in the light”

He is well aware of contemporary, political, national and international issues. He reads the newspapers and keeps his knowledge up-to-date. His sound general knowledge has won his a respectable place. He is worthy of being the best friend. In short, He bears all the high standard qualities. I hope he will remain in my life. In form of a true friend who loves, helps and sacrifices his interests without expecting anything in return. I am proud of him. May our friendship abide forever! As it is said:

"Choices define who we are and Choices make friends."
