My House

“The house of everyone to him is a castle, a fortress as well as his defense against injury and enemies.”

House is the most fundamental and radical need of life. It gives human a sense of safety and security. It makes life easy, comfortable and enjoyable. No animate thing can live without house birds and animals also make their shelters. Birds may fly whole the day but come back to their nests. Animals may roam whole the day in search grazing but come back to their rest places. Honey bees have honey combs. Snakes and even ants have their holes. Every living thing has an innate desire for an abode. It is said,

“Home is a place where life begins to wobble.”

My house is situated on the bank of a big canal is incredibly splendid huge shady trees are surrounding the white building of my house. Its environment is quiet and calm. It stands away from the hue and cry of city life. Its building is wonderfully clean and spacious. It is said,

“East or west home is the best.”

We built our house five years ago Professional architectures have made its map. We took special care about the building material used its construction. The covered area of my house is “800” yards. The whole house is airy and sunny. It has five bedrooms, two guest rooms, a drawing room, a TV lounge, a dining hall, a kitchen, a study room and a vast courtyard. As it is said,

“A comfortable house is a great source of happiness.”

The walls of my house are painted white. The beautiful white slabs of marble are fixed on the floors. The doors and windows are made of durable wood. The whole house is furnished with beautiful furniture “Persia rugs” on the floor of drawing look elegant. Some pictures are hanging on the walls drawing room. They are very classical and stunning. Two beautiful antique swords are also hanging there.

Our neighbors are very friendly. The environment of house is very peaceful. We are a happy family. I find peace in my house. It is said,

“He is the happiest be a king or peasant who finds peace in his house.”

A green front lawn and back garden of my house are particularly beautiful. The thick green “mistletoe” covering the front wall of my house enhances the beauty of my house. The soft, tidy and trimmed grass of the front lawn looks like the green velvety floor. In the back garden of my house. There is mango, lemon, orange and banana trees. They bear plentiful fruits. Their bloom flowers of many colors in the front lawn. Their fragrance spreads all around. In evening I sit in my garden. The fragrance of flowers refreshens my mind. In morning dew drops on the grass look like pearls. I feel very happy while sitting in my lawn. As it is said,

“Blessed is the house upon whose walls the shade of an old tree softly flaks.”

I also give the flowers of my lawn to my friends as gifts. To me my house is paradise.

“To Adam paradise was home, to good among his descendant’s home is paradise.”

It does not matter you live in a house of gold or bricks, to everyone his house is a place of peace and love. I like my house very much. To me it is paradise. I find peace in my house. I pray to Allah Almighty.

“May Allah bless my home and it remain ever and forever!”
