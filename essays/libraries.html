Libraries

“Library is a delivery room, an origin and a nexus where ideas take birth.”

The word library comes from the Latin word "liber", meaning book. A library is, as everyone knows, a room or building in which a well-arranged collection of books, CDs, newspapers, gramophone records, etc. is kept for reading or reference. A library is a shed of knowledge. It is a proper place where everyone can find some books that are full of knowledge. This is an age of progress in education. Great importance is attached to the libraries as they play a vital role in promoting literacy and education.

“The true university these days is a collection of books.”

Libraries are real wealth of a nation. They are ways to progress and prosperity. They encourage the nation towards research. They enhance the nations and outlooks of public. They play vital role to a nation’s progress.

Libraries are places where people come to fulfil the thirst of knowledge. Library is a place which contain books and many other informational materials. People come to library for reading, study and reference. A library contains a variety of stuff which is related to fields of education and information.

Libraries serve nations. They provide a platform of open knowledge for public. They have a value able record of present, past and future. As it is said:

“Without libraries you have no past and no future.”

Libraries also preserve culture of a nation. The values and civilization of a nation are represented by their libraries. Libraries are living evidences that how is determined a nation.

Libraries are science labs grooming ideas. They make the mindsets broader. A person of a single book belongs to none. More reading makes mindsets mature and peace loving. Books are message of peace. They are the most loyal and sincere friends. The world spread upon pages is reflection of the real world. As it is said:

“Libraries are hospitals of mind. A person reading a book is actually in all quarters of the wide world.”

Libraries are particularly essential in educational institutions. Every school, college or a university must have a markable library. Students use to go to library for reading books and to research different topics and fields. Libraries enhance the class experiences of the students. They induce both academic and co-curricular factors.

“Libraries are our friends.”

They introduce good habits of reading in students. Public officials also use libraries to sort social and official complexities. People of every field are linked to libraries. But it is a fact that no library has all the information and knowledge. So, different libraries should be set up at different scales. Libraries from an interaction between people of different fields and generations.

“Libraries are places, a place where lofty spirits of all nations and generations meet.”

Libraries are basically, divided into six basic categories. First, there are public libraries. They serve the general members of public. Second, there are school libraries. They serve students and faculty through the high school level. Third, there are college and university libraries. They serve students in higher education. Fourth, there are research libraries. They meet the needs of advanced scholars. Fifth, there are special libraries. They help various (organizations), industries and governmental agencies. Sixth, there are government libraries. They serve governmental departments and agencies. Libraries play an important role in propagation of education. Therefore, libraries should be set up on a large scale.

Libraries have many hidden worlds inside it. Unseen realities of world and society are discovered here. They are way forward to new inventions and development. They serve nations and are vital part of a civilization. So, government should pay special heed to maintain good libraries to make the society better and progressive. Government should provide specific funds for libraries and people should develop a good habit of visiting libraries. As it is said:

“Libraries are our friends.”

Books are collection of ideas and libraries are collection of books. So, there is no better place than a library to be loved.
