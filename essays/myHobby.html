My Hobby

“A hobby a day keeps the doldrums away.”
(Phyllis McGinley)

Today, man lives in a world where struggle and effort are necessary for survival. He has to toil from dawn to dusk Physical and mental exertions leave him utterly exhausted and enervated after the whole day's work. The daily routine life rusts his talent and efficiency.

The word hobby is derived from hobby horse which is a stick fitted wooden horse and on which small children ride on for their amusement. Hobby is something that we do mere for our joy amusement and delight. According to sir Wickson Hobby is defined as:

“Alternative mind interest”

This is the word of struggle and efforts. Man has two toils whole the day for his survival. He usually gets tired office labour. He needs some pleasant activity to lessen the burden of this boredom. Such an activities called hobby. As it is said:

“In our leisure we reveal, what kind of people we are”

Hobby is an interesting pursuit through which man derives pleasure recreation and amusement. In hobby man sucks pleasures feels exalted mentally elated. It is said:

“Hobby gives a stimulus to better living”

There are many hobbies like stamp collecting, coin collecting, reading books and watching TV. One adopts hobby according to his taste and interest. Different people have different hobbies but my hobby is gardening. It is said:

“Allah almighty first planted a garden and indeed it is the purist of human pleasure”

This hobby is a source of joy and pleasure for me. I have made a lawn in the front and a garden at the back of my house. In the garden there are many kinds of flowers bloom. It is said:

“Where flowers bloom so does hope”

These flowers show different colors. They give off sweet smell and present a beautiful view. Green trees, grassy lawn and flowers of many colors add to the beauty of my house. It is said:

“To plant a garden is to believe in tomorrow”

The fluttering leaves and dancing branches attract the viewers. So:

“Won’t you come to my garden. I would my like roses to see you.”

I feel happy when I hear the songs of the birds sitting in the branches of these trees. A fountain plays in the middle of this garden. The green grass of the lawn also looks beautiful. When I walk on the grass wet with dew drops, I feel fresh and happy. The dew drops sparkling in the sunshine look like pearls. It is said:

“The best plays to seek Allah is in a garden. You can dig for him there.”

Gardening provides me a good exercise for the day. It keeps me healthy, fresh and happy for labour of the day. Gardening is my sincere friend; it inspires me whenever I am in depressed mood. I cast a glance on my garden and my blossoms.

“You can bury a lot of troubles by digging in the dirt”

I daily devote some of my time for the maintenance of my garden. I dig the beds of flowers, trim the grass and apply manure to the plants. Sometime, I spray plants and prune the flowers. I love to work and sit in my garden and lawn. It is rightly said:

“In every gardener there is a child, who believes in the seed fairy.”

In evening, when I sit in my garden, I feel fresh and happy. It is said:

“One is nearer to Allah heart in a garden than anywhere else on the earth.”

My garden is a source of joy and peace for me. I also participate actively in plantation scheme of the government every year. I have planted hundreds of trees at different places throughout the city. After planting these trees, I also take care of them.

“Gardening is a matter of your enthusiasm holding up until your back gets used to it”

So, hobby should be regarded just as a mental recreation.
