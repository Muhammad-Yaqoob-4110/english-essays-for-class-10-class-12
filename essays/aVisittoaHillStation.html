A Visit to a Hill Station

“Travelling in childhood part of education, in the elder is a part of experience."

Recreational trips and tours are part and parcel of life. They enhance our knowledge and experience. They provide us with pleasure and joy. They refresh our minds. They add to our understanding.

“On Earth there is no heaven but there are pieces of it."

It is a great pleasure to visit a hill station in summer. It was a pleasant day of summer when we proceeded to Murree. During the journey to the Murree hills, we enjoyed the beautiful scenes all around. There were tall green trees growing along the road on the slopes of hills. The clouds were playing hide seek with the mountain peaks. Soon it began drizzling.

It was a pleasant and memorable journey. Reaching Murree, we stayed in a hotel. First of all, we took a bath, enjoyed lunch and took Some rest. In the evening, we got ready to see the beautiful hills of Murree. It was really a beautiful sight. The cool breeze was fanning our faces and refreshing our minds.

"The best to pay for a lovely moment is to enjoy it."

We climbed the mountains and ran wildly all over the hill. It was nearly getting dark when we returned. A gentle shower of rain began to fall. We hurried back and reached our hotel. Then we changed our dresses as it had been quite cold and wet due to rain. The night was cold I had to cover myself with a blanket Throughout the night it continued raining but it was dawning the rain stopped. We were glad to see this sudden change of weather.

"Nature holds the key our aesthetic, intellectual, cognitive and even spiritual satisfaction."

We planned to visit Patriata. After an hour's journey, we reached there. We bought tickets for cable car. It was novel experience for us. It was an amazing experience to look dawn from the cable car. The view beneath simply seemed to be a fairyland. At last, we reached Patriata Hills. We stayed there till 5 p.m. We rode on horses and drank tea. We also enjoyed а delicious lunch. After this we took some photographs of the beautiful scenes. On our way back to Murree we stayed for some time in the Wonderland Park. We reached Murree at night.

"To travel is to take a journey into yourself."

The next day we visited adjacent valleys. We went out to see the Sanitorium down in the valley. There were thick forests of pine trees and the entire area was green and charming. There are also many fruit gardens on the slopes. On our way back we crossed two fast-flowing streams. The water of these streams was very cold and clean. The number of glowing colors looked as much beautiful as:

"The best and most beautiful things in the world cannot be seen or even touched. They must be felt with heart."

(Helen Keller)
So,

"Time will fly, Time will change but those memories always stay.”

We stayed there for three days. Then we returned to our city. We had a lot of golden memories of this memorable trip. Our photographs would keep this trip ever fresh in our minds. We gained a lot of knowledge, experience from recreation and experience from this trip.

“I visited many places, Some of them quite exotic and far away, But I always returned to myself chockfull with memories.”

(D. Stojanovic)
